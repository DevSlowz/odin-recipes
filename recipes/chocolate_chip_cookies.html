Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Enjoy freshly baked Chocolate Chip Cookies, a timeless treat loved by kids and adults alike. These cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with gooey chocolate chips in every bite. Whether for a snack, dessert, or special occasion, these cookies are sure to delight.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter, softened
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream together butter and sugars until smooth.
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients until well combined.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop dough by spoonfuls onto baking sheets.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
